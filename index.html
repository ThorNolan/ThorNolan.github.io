ABOUT ME

My name is Thor Nolan, and I am an aspiring web developer living in the Bay Area. I graduated from Reed College in 2016 and am currently enrolled in UC Berkeley Extension's coding bootcamp. I'm always looking for new ways to expand my coding skill set and gain experience working with code any way that I can.


My areas of interest include technology, philosophy, politics, writing, and gaming, but that's to name only a few. By the time I finish my bootcamp, I will have significant hands-on experience working with HTML, CSS and CSS frameworks, vanilla Javascript, jQuery, and React, Git, Github, and Heroku, Django, MySQL and MongoDB, as well as Express and the MERN Stack.


When I'm not working on honing my coding skills, I enjoy rock climbing at my local climbing gym, or getting outside to climb or hike whenever I can. I am also an avid PC gamer, and have enjoyed building and updating the parts of my own PC for gaming as well as adding to my unreasonably large (and still-growing) library of games.
